Welcome to Enzyme Furnitures

We are Italy’s leading manufacturer of bespoke luxury furniture and handcrafted luxury sofas.

Our luxury furniture collections

We are dedicated to fine craftsmanship and superior lead times, our entire luxury furniture collection is handmade and fully customizable, to meet your unique requirements. All of our frames come with a 15-year guarantee.

We pride ourselves on our tailored service; creating made to measure pieces that adhere to our clients' specifications to the letter. Our commitment to quality extends to every area of the design and production process; sourcing only the finest quality materials for our bespoke furniture creations. We are able to take bespoke furniture from initial concept to completion and delivery in less than 6 weeks.
